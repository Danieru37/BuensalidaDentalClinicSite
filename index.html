About the dentist


Dr. Ma. Zarina Buensalida

A graduate of Bachelor of Science in Dentistry at Centro Escolar University (CEU). A member of the Kalookan City Dental Chapter (KCDC) and an affiliate of Filipino-Chinese Dental Chapter. Dr. Zarina is a loving mother and an experienced dentist that has been in practice for 27 years and counting.

She is dedicated to her craft and is ready to provide you with quality service that would guarantee to make you smile. At Buensalida Dental Clinic, we provide comprehensive and compassionate dental care to patients of all ages. We offer a wide variety of services from preventive care to cosmetic treatment.

"Your dental health and well-being are our top priorities."

Services


Preventive

Oral prophylaxix, pits and fissure sealants, fluoride treatments, education and awareness, dietary choices


Restoration

Fillings, crown and bridges, root canal


Prosthetic

Dentures, oral splints, night guard


Orthodontict

Malocclusion, braces, clear aligners, retainers, orthognathic surgery, extraoral and intraoral appliance


Oral Surgery

Tooth extraction, wisdom tooth removal, biopsies, cleft lip and palate surgery, TMJ disorders, oral cancer treatment


Radiography

Periapical, panoramic, CBCT scans (referral)


Cosmetic

Teeth whitening, dental bonding, veneers, gum contouring, inlays and onlays, smile makeover
